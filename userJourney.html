User Journey For E-claim
Registering new claim - Click on file tab.
Enter valid policy/certificate number along with incident date.
Select the type of claim.
File in the claim details form (enter all mandatory details).
Upload the required documents as per the type of claim.
After submitting the claim, the customer will receive an email with mandatory list of documents.
Tracking the claim status - click on the track claim tab.
Uploading documents - click on claim status and click on the claim number in which document needs to be uploaded.
